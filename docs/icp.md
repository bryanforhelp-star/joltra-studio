# Joltra Studio — Ideal Customer Profile

**One line:** Creators who monetize through expertise, publish long-form regularly on one platform, and leave distribution on the table by ignoring secondary platforms.

**Volume mindset:** Outreach is a numbers game. Target **50+ prospects per research run**, mix of smaller (5K–30K) and mid-size (30K–150K) creators. Don't only hunt big names.

---

## Follower sweet spots

| Tier | Followers | Priority |
|------|-----------|----------|
| **Micro** | 3K–15K | High — hungry, decision-maker, often no team |
| **Growing** | 15K–75K | Highest — proven content, clear gaps, can afford retainer |
| **Established** | 75K–200K | Good — but verify no social team |
| **Skip** | Under 3K or over 500K | Too early or likely has team |

## Anti-personas (hard skip)

| Anti-persona | Why skip |
|--------------|----------|
| **Fan page / aggregator** | Not original creator (e.g. celebrity repost channels) |
| Entertainment-only | No business reason to expand |
| Social team / manager | Can't make the decision |
| Short-form only | Nothing to repurpose |
| Active on 4+ platforms | No gap to sell |

## Scoring (max 12, DM if ≥ 7)

| Factor | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Proven creator (3K+, weekly long-form) | 2 (1 if 3K–8K) |
| Distribution gap (2+ dormant) | 2 (1 if only 1) |
| Buyer signal | 2 |
| Original creator (not aggregator) | required |
| Content repurposability | 1 |
| Recent activity (<30 days) | 1 |
| Contactable | 1 |
| No red flags | 1 |
| Specific content reference + praise | 1 |
| Persona match | 1 |

Hard disqualify: aggregator/fan page, zero dormant platforms, under 3K followers.
