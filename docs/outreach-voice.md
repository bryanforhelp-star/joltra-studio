# Outreach voice — Joltra creator DMs

How we write cold outreach. Read this before drafting DMs.

**No voice file exists in the repo yet** — if you have a personal tone doc or sample DMs that worked (UGC outreach, etc.), add it as `docs/outreach-voice-samples.md` and reference it here.

---

## Core principle: observational, not performative

We did research. We noticed specific things. We say what we saw — we do **not** pretend to be a long-time fan.

### Never say
- "I've been following you for a while"
- "Love your content" (with nothing specific)
- "I'm a big fan"
- Anything you cannot verify from one research session

### Always do
- Reference **one specific piece** by title (video, episode, post)
- Say **what you actually noticed** about it (topic, format, takeaway, who it's for)
- Call out the **platform gap** with evidence (last post date, empty channel, etc.)
- Offer **what we'd do** — not vague "AI system" jargon

---

## Structure (primary DM)

1. **Hook** — "Hey [name] — was looking at your [platform] and came across [specific title]."
2. **Observation** — One true sentence about what stood out (teaching style, niche, a moment, consistency).
3. **Gap** — "You're active on [main] but [gap platforms] are basically empty — feels like reach you're leaving on the table."
4. **Offer** — Repurpose existing long-form into clips + posts. No new filming. Flat retainer.
5. **CTA** — Soft ask: worth a quick rundown of week one?

---

## Tone
- Short sentences. Sounds like a person texting, not a marketing email.
- Confident but not salesy. No "crushing it", no "game-changer", no "in today's fast-paced world."
- Direct. If you're not sure about something, don't claim it.

---

## Example (good)

> Hey Sarah — was looking at your YouTube and pulled up "How I plan my week in Notion." The walkthrough format is super clear for people who already use Notion but feel scattered.
>
> You're posting weekly on YouTube but TikTok and LinkedIn are basically empty — last LinkedIn post was 8 months ago. That's a lot of clip-ready content sitting in one place.
>
> We take long-form like that and turn it into platform-native clips + posts for the channels you're not on. You keep filming on YouTube. Flat monthly retainer.
>
> Want me to sketch what week one would look like from that video?

## Example (bad)

> Hey Sarah, I've been following your YouTube for a while — your content is amazing! I run an AI system that takes your existing content and turns it into platform-ready clips...

---

## Follow-ups
- **3-day:** Offer a free sample batch from a **specific** video you already named. No pressure.
- **7-day:** Last note. Door open. No guilt trip.
